ABOUT US

JANET AND JOHN
COLLEGE

The school was established on the 15th September, 2003 as a partnership venture by Mr. and Mrs John Monwuba and Mr. and Mrs Godwin Obu. The school is located at No 1,Estaport Avenue, Soluyi in Gbagada area of Lagos State. The school aim at providing an environment where students will be made to imbibe the spirit of hard work, discipline and righteousness in accordance with its motto: work hard and keep straight The school is a co educational institution open to children (boys and girls) between the ages of ten and twelve who must have completed or about to complete their last year in primary school i.e primary and basic six.The school also aim at developing the potentialities of individuals on the intellectual , physical and spiritual planes,in an atmosphere where differences are recognized and respected and where creativity is encouraged. We are to use tested and proven human resources, in a controlled learning environment to achieve academics, moral and social excellence.

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

The board of Janet&John College have a genuine intrest in trainig the intellectual,Moral and physical characteristics of children so they will be able to enjoy living as children,support themselves adequately when they become adults and ultimately contribute to nation building The children would have been prepared for tertiary education through developing communication skills developing creativity and critical thinking and finally eliminating learning difficulties arising from home environment and cultural Background
