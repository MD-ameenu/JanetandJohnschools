LOCATION

The school is located at 1, Estaport Avenue, Soluyi-Gbagada area of Lagos State, Nigeria. The school is a co-educational institution open for admission to children (boys and girls) between the ages of 10 and 12 who must have completed or about to complete their last year in primary school i.e. Primary/Basic 6. Admission is also based on performance in an open and competitive entrance examination plus written/oral interview. Students can also be admitted into JSS2, SS1 and SS2 subject to their satisfactory performance in a qualifying examination. Finally, admission is also open to children who have completed their Basic 6 irrespective of ethnicity, religion or creed.
